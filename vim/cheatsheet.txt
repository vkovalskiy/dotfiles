-  <minus>		[count] lines upward, on the first non-blank
			character |linewise|.

_  <underscore>		[count] - 1 lines downward, on the first non-blank
			character |linewise|.

+		or					*+*
CTRL-M		or					*CTRL-M* *<CR>*
<CR>			[count] lines downward, on the first non-blank
			character |linewise|.
